THE POWER OF NATURE
By Anshita Gera

Once, my brain became galaxy's expanse,
And my heart had earthquake by circumstance.
When concurrently, my flooded eyes gazed upon room's artificial stars,
They took me behind my self- developed bars,
To my beautiful history of dusk till dawn,
And the scintillating sweetness of surroundings to dwell on.

The cherry blossom, the bird of paradise,
The pinks, the purples and the yellows intensifies.
The blue waves combined with the golden thread of charm,
Oh lord, natural food, water and medicine on our palm.
Sitting on the rock, scrutinising the nature,
Admiring spirited seeds, trees and every creature.

My brain, body and heart have become constant companions.
I am now a shooting star, looking for constellations.
No longer dejected, but cheerful like a rainbow,
To all those in affliction: Go with the nature's eternal flow!
Go with the nature's eternal flow!
