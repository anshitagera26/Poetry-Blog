About the Poetry Blog

The purpose is simple: to live and deliver.

This blog is not only about poems but also the feelings behind them. In today's world, emotions and mental health play a very important role in everyone's life as it's a mixture of ups and downs. What matters is how we acknowledge those emotions. This is a platform to let out the feelings. Here, the poems can change someone's mindset if he/she reads. However, to bring about the change in people and reality, express your sentiments through poems, and join the platform!
