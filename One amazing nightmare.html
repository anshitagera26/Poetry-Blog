ONE AMAZING NIGHTMARE
By Anshita Gera

A beautiful morning came with a weird warning,
A day to showcase her skills of performing;
With many choices, she had to choose one,
To make the future wonderful, like none!!
Overthinking and researching, she was bewildered.
Her creative supervision turned into blurred smog filtered.
All the pros and cons were not best fitted,
And the ray of hope wasn't even emitted.
Passing everyday the twenty four hour routine,
She remained only the perplexed teen.

A dreadful night with a worst nightmare:
Her future was dim everywhere,
Taking loans and having life taking thought,
The bed of dried roses, she had caught!
Sweat came upon her and she came upon sweat.
Woke up, became silent and gave load on mindset.
Pondered what she actually wanted, with a cup of tea;
Enlightened that writing was her cup of tea!
Years have been passed, became a renowned writer,
That nightmare changed her life, she became much lighter!
